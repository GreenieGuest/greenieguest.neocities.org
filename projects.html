EWOW Response Verifier

January 2025

A tool I made mainly for EWOW. Put in any response and see various information/stats about it in one place, including verbosity, acronyms, parenthesis, an unofficial meta score, and most importantly word count and previews on a voting screen!


EWOWordle

July 2025

A primitive clone of Squirdle, except with EWOW statistics. Uses rolling leaderboard ranks, life counts and total scores.


Bracketify

June 2025

I sometimes like to 'discover music' by taking songs I haven't listened to from my favorite Spotify artists and pitting them against each other in a tournament bracket. Now you can do the same, except with most popular songs!
