Universalis, 1330 AD

(made 1/17/2022, original map made 12/16/2019)

One of my favorite memories of all time is playing an on-paper alternate history grand campaign for most of 8th grade (2019) with my friend group, none of whom were map nerds or history nerds. All 6 (occasionally 7) of us picked a nation and slowly grew as history progressed, and by taking creative liberties and forming hegemonies, all of us had fun over drawing on a map of Europe. This is what Europe looked like in the year 1330.


Berlogoes Islands

(made 9/25/2021)

Terrain map of my fictional world, Berlogoes, consisting of 5 main continents/islands. The island circle concept came from a map I drew in 4th grade, and the lore grew over the last 10 years of my life, now including 31 sovereign nations, 200 city names and 1000 years of feudal history. I even made my own personal EU4 mod for it!
