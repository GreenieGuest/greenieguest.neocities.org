~ Welcome ~

Hello, welcome to my personal website! This is the site I use to showcase all of my personal interests such as music, modding and other things I do such as my Youtube Channel and development. I also showcase some of my personal projects on here.

I'm using Neocities as other people I knew (LegitSi) had it in their bio, I have always wanted my own site (and my own forum) and have used some webpage hosting sites before. The layout of this site is inspired by ProBoards containers because I loved the aesthetic from a forum 'Netski' I ran in the early 2020s.

This site basically serves as a hub for all things GreenieGuest. Have fun exploring around!

Don't expect the Holy Grail of websites, I'm still somewhat new to HTML. (If it ain't broke, don't fix it.)
